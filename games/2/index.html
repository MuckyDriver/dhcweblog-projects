Traffic

Watch as vehicles on the highway pass you and disappearing off into the distance. Cars and trucks will drive along the road at different speeds depending if they are in the fast lane or not.

About Game

Traffic is a basic showcase game that simulates cars passing on highway. The reason for this game is unknown but it was fun to make as I was playing around with perspective in video game map. I also developed a dynamic lighting script which can be used in any Roblox experience to give the graphics something interesting - this script basically adjusts the lighting depending if you are looking at the sun or under an object usually inside a building etc.
